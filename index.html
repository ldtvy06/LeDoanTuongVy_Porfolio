Veilmark

A B2B SCHOOL MERCHANDISE IDENTITY SYSTEM

Safety Symbol School Branding

The Tension

Many schools use customized merchandise systems to manage student belongings while reinforcing school identity and consistency.

They use visible names to identify children's belongings quickly and efficiently.

However, public name visibility may unintentionally expose children's personal information outside school environments.

Real-World Observation

This insight emerged after seeing a parenting video where a mother shared why she stopped printing her child's name on personal belongings after a stranger called the child correctly in public.

The child immediately responded — highlighting how familiar cues such as names can unintentionally create a false sense of trust and familiarity.

Identification creates convenience —
but also visibility.

Consumer Insight

Schools and parents both want kids' names on merch —

schools for identification, parents for personalization —

but parents fear it's also a stranger's first introduction to their child.

"Risk elimination is unrealistic. Risk reduction through controlled visibility is achievable."

VEILMARK – A B2B School Merchandise Identity System

A Dual Identity System

Public-facing layerSymbol-based identification

Internal layerStudent name / ID information

With this system, the risk of children's information being leaked is significantly reduced.

Design Principles — The 3S Framework
Safety

Protecting children's information through controlled visibility.

Symbol

Creating recognizable and emotionally engaging personal identifiers.

School Branding

Building unified and differentiated school identity systems.

Strategic Value

Schools – Branding + differentiation
Parents – Safety + peace of mind
Children – Recognition + belonging

Go-to-Market Strategy

Target Partners

  • Private preschools
  • Kindergartens
  • Education centers
  • Primary schools

⇒ Targeting schools seeking stronger branding consistency and modernized student merchandise systems.

Rollout Approach

01 – Pilot

Launch with selected classrooms using themed identity systems.

02 — Feedback

Collect parent, teacher, and operational feedback.

03 — Scale

Expand into school-wide merchandise and branding systems.

*Long-term opportunity: School-wide identity ecosystems

Beyond Identification

Veilmark rethinks how schools identify children by balancing:

  • safer recognition
  • emotional personalization
  • operational convenience
  • school branding

FINAL STATEMENT

We help schools build a stronger brand identity while keeping every child safely recognized.

Designed as a strategic business concept for modern school identity systems.
